What is Fantasy Football?

Fantasy sports are games in which you, as the owner, draft a team of players from real players in the sports league to create your own “fantasy” team. You compete against other owners in your league, made up of family, friends, or strangers, to score points based on the performance of your players in the actual games. This website will analyze Fantasy (American) Football statistics using Tableau to create data visualizations of trends over time.

Join League

Gather your friends or family to create a league or join an existing league.

Draft Team

Prior to the start of the season, draft real players to create your team.

Adjust Lineup Weekly

Change your lineup based on injuries, bye weeks, and matchups to maximize points.
