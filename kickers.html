What is a Kicker (K)?

On a Football team, the Kicker is a player on special teams whose role is to kick field goals, extra points, and kickoffs. Kickers line up behind the offensive line positioned about 7 - 8 yards behind the line of scrimmage so the ball can be snapped and held before being kicked in between the two goal posts. You may only have one active kicker playing on your Fantasy Football team at one time.

How does a Kicker earn Fantasy Football points?

Kickers players typically have low scoring potential given their limited time on the field and lowest potential point accrual. Each time a kicker kicks the ball, the player will earn positive, negative, or no points for your Fantasy Football team. Positive points are awarded based on points earned by the kick (1 extra point or 3 for a field goal), the distance of the field goal, and more. Points are taken away from your Fantasy Football team for this position only when kicks are blocked. No points are awarded if the kick does not result in the ball going through the goal posts.
